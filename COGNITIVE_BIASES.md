| Bias                              | What It Is                              | How to Recognize in YOU                                        | How to Recognize in OTHERS                      | Quick Counter                              |
|-----------------------------------|-----------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| **Confirmation Bias**             | Favoring info that supports your belief | Feeling “this confirms it” quickly; not exploring alternatives | Selective data use; ignoring opposing views     | *“What would prove me wrong?”*             |
| **Anchoring Bias**                | Over-relying on first info              | First idea feels “right”; hard to reframe                      | “We’ve always done this”; early estimates stick | *“If we started fresh, what would we do?”* |
| **Availability Bias**             | Overweighting recent/vivid events       | Recent issue feels overly important                            | “This just happened, so it’s critical”          | *“Is this frequent or just memorable?”*    |
| **Loss Aversion**                 | Fear of loss > desire for gain          | Over-cautious decisions; avoiding risk                         | Resistance to change; “what if this breaks?”    | *“What’s the cost of not acting?”*         |
| **Fundamental Attribution Error** | Blaming people, not systems             | “They are difficult/incompetent”                               | “That team is the problem”                      | *“What system is causing this?”*           |
| **Halo Effect**                   | One positive trait biases judgment      | Over-trusting strong performers                                | Senior voices go unchallenged                   | *“Is the idea strong on its own?”*         |
| **Horn Effect**                   | One negative trait biases judgment      | Dismissing ideas from certain people                           | Certain people’s ideas ignored                  | *“Am I rejecting the idea or the person?”* |
| **Status Quo Bias**               | Preference for current state            | Defaulting to existing solutions                               | “Why change this?”                              | *“Would we design this today?”*            |
| **Sunk Cost Fallacy**             | Continuing due to past investment       | “We’ve already spent so much…”                                 | Protecting failing projects                     | *“Would we start this today?”*             |
| **Overconfidence Bias**           | Overestimating accuracy                 | Jumping to solutions quickly; high certainty                   | “This will definitely work”                     | *“What am I missing?”*                     |
| **Groupthink**                    | Harmony > critical thinking             | Avoiding dissent                                               | Quick consensus; no pushback                    | *“What would a critic say?”*               |
| **Authority Bias**                | Overweighting senior opinions           | Deferring to leaders too easily                                | Leadership ideas not challenged                 | *“What does the data say?”*                |
| **Bandwagon Effect**              | Following the majority                  | Aligning quickly with group                                    | “Everyone thinks this”                          | *“What if everyone is wrong?”*             |
